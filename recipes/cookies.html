Cookies Recipe

Cookies come in all shapes, sizes, and flavors — but one thing they have in common is they depend on the right ingredients, techniques, and tools to make them the best they can be. Because there are so many different kinds of cookies, we can offer general guidelines on how to make cookies — but you should always follow the instructions for the particular cookie recipe you're making.

Let's get straight to our top 10 tips for making better cookies, then we'll look at how to make cookie dough and we'll trouble-shoot common cookie-making problem. Finally, we'll take a deeper dive into choosing the right cookie ingredients and highlight some essential cooking-making tools you'll need.

Ingriedients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Baking Soda
  • One egg
  1. Cream the butter with a mixer
  2. Cream the butter with the sugar and egg
  3. Add flour and baking soda to the creamed butter and sugar
  4. Add Vanilla Extract and continue
  5. Chill Dough for at least one day
  6. Bake at 350F for at least 10 minutes
